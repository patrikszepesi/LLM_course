Aligned Vectors(bank)

A1⋅B1=1⋅2+2⋅4=2+8=10 A_1 \cdot B_1 = 1 \cdot 2 + 2 \cdot 4 = 2 + 8 = 10 A1​⋅B1​=1⋅2+2⋅4=2+8=10
